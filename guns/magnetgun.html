Magnet Gun

The Magnet Gun is a unique weapon used to attract and hold metal objects. When the fire button is held, the closest metal object will be attracted. Swords, Sledgehammers and similar objects can be thrown from the Magnet Gun when the fire button is released. Rocks can also be attracted by the Magnet Gun. Mines that have been placed can be safely moved if the Magnet Gun picks them up. Ducks armored with the Chestplate can be grabbed by the Magnet Gun, immobilizing them. The Magnet Gun can be used against walls to move a player wielding it, this allows for wall climbing, faster movement and reaching otherwise difficult to reach areas.

See Magnet Techniques for more information.
