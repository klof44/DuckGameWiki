Elon Musket

The Musket is a long range, high spread rifle which requires a long reload between shots. During reloads, the player is immobilized in an animation which takes 4 seconds, this leads to most players throwing away the rifle after one shot. When jumping or falling pressing reload will not reload the rifle. When shot, a puff of smoke is produced and some players will ragdoll themselves in the smoke to fake their own deaths. The musket is often seen with the Blunderbuss and the Old Pistol, because of their similar abilities and color palette.

Repeatedly pressing the fire button will make the reload slightly faster, to 2.5 seconds, this can catch players off guard if you use the smoke screen to reload and they are not paying attention to you.

Old-timey rifle, takes approximately 150 years to reload.
