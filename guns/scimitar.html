Energy Scimitar

The Energy Scimitar is a powerful melee weapon. The Energy Scimitar can be charged by dragging it on the ground (by shortly moving down and sideways at the same time) which will make the player run faster, it then allows players to: jump higher/double jump or strike/attack with great reach when pressing attack.

When charged and thrown the Energy Scimitar flies straight and fast in that direction, when it hits a wall it will stick to it and can act as a step for climbing. If thrown downwards, your duck will be sent upwards at great heights. By timing your attack just right or simply spamming it, you can reflect shots from most guns. If a bullet from a Quad Laser is hit by the sword, it'll turn into a powerful Death Ray-like laser beam.

How do you invent a sword? It uses modern technology.
