Saxophone

The Saxophone is a musical instrument in Duck Game. It can be played by using the attack button, pitch can be varied by pressing left trigger (or L2) when using a gamepad controller. If the jam button is pressed (default: Home), the Saxophone can be played by pressing number keys and backspace on a keyboard.

Crave the dulcet tones of smooth jazz? This is the item for you.
