Wump Net Gun

The Wump Net Gun was a Removed gun in Duck Game that shot nets forward in an arc similar to the Grenade Launcher. Ideally the gun would have shot backwards as the intended use and functionality allowed the user to launch themselves in spiked nets toward other ducks behind them to kill them.
