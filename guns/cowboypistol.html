Cowboy Pistol

Characteristics of the Cowboy pistol are similar to the Magnum with the same penetration and range but without the recoil and cooldown the magnum has. The pistol can be aimed at any angle with the right control stick of a gamepad or by holding the left click of your mouse and moving it around your duck.

In the level editor it is described as "OFFLINE ONLY".

OFFLINE ONLY

Standard issue .44 Magnum. Pretty great for killing things, really great for killing things that are trying to hide. Watch the kick, unless you're trying to shoot the ceiling.
