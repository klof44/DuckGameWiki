Cork Gun

The Cork Gun is an unused item which shoots a cork which is fixed to the gun with a rope. The gun must be reloaded by holding the shoot button until the cork fully retracts back into the gun, at which point it can be fired again. The Cork Gun will pass through and kill other ducks and continue for the entirety of it's duration or until it hits a wall.
