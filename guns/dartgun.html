Dart Gun

The Dart Gun is modeled after a Nerf gun and shoots darts. If another player is hit with a dart they are knocked back, if they have a weapon it is disarmed from them. Darts can be stood on if shot on a wall, this allows for wall climbing if the player does this successively. Darts can trigger buttons, and mines. Darts do not knock back ragdolled players.

If the Dart Gun is in contact with fire, the tip of the gun will light on fire. When the tip is on fire and the Dart Gun is shot, flaming darts will be launched, if these collide with players they will be lit on fire. After a fixed amount of time, the tip will melt and the dartgun will no longer fire flaming darts, but will return to firing normal darts. Flaming darts will also be created if a dart flies through fire.

Shoots like a gun, feels like a gentle breeze.
