Net Gun

The Net Gun is one of the more common weapons found on many official maps. When shot, the Net Gun releases a net that will immobilize a player for a short time if they are caught. Trapped players can wiggle in the net, which reduces the time the player is trapped. A trapped player acts as a Rock or Box and can kill other players if it lands on their head. Other players also gain an opportunity to pick them up and chuck them off of the map or into an environmental hazard, or kill them via any other means while they're unable to move. Netting a duck that is holding a Sword will usually kill them instantly, due to the Sword falling from their grip.

See Net Gun Techniques for more information.
