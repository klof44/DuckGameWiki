Matchbox

The Matchbox is an ineffective flame weapon found mostly in Random Item Boxes and Presents.

A small flame is shot out when the fire button is pressed, if this flame hits another player they are set ablaze.

Sometimes the Matchbox will release two flames for a single button press. When heated up the Matchbox will explode into 5 fire particles fired randomly.

A box full of fire sticks. Keep away from children.

Category Fire
Ammo Type Lit Matches
Firing Mode Semi-Automatic
Spread Medium
Direction Straight
Range 1-2 tiles
Capacity 20
Console Name matchbox

Bugs

Fire particles spawned by the Matchbox can spawn inside its collision box sometimes heating it up and explode on the player firing it, making it randomly explode when used.
