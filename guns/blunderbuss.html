Blunderbuss

The Blunderbuss is effectively a Shotgun with a long reload animation between shots and a smoke screen. The high spread with multiple pellets makes the blunderbuss a more attractive option than the Musket at close range which is often seen on the same maps. During reloads, the player is immobilized in an animation which takes 4 seconds, this leads to many players throwing away the weapon after one shot.

Repeatedly pressing the fire button will make the reload slightly faster, to 2.5 seconds, this can catch players off guard if you use the smoke screen to reload and they are not paying attention to you.

Old-timey shotgun, takes approximately 150 years to reload.
