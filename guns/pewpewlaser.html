Pew Pew Laser

The Pew Pew Laser is a three-round burst laser weapon found typically on futuristic themed maps. When firing the three-round burst there is a small amount of spread between them typically enough to hit a player in all positions.

The long range and three-round burst make this weapon strong on most maps, although the slow travel speed is its main weakness.

The gun will still fire its three rounds if you toss it while it shoots. Depending on the timing, you can hit other players with it that way.

Quick-fire laser beam of ULTIMATE DESTRUCTION... with an adorable wittle name.
