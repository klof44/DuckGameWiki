Mag Blaster

The Mag Blaster is a powerful, long range futuristic gun with the ability to instantly destroy Doors. With the long range and semi-automatic nature of the Mag Blaster, it is considered a powerful weapon. It may be difficult to control the Mag Blaster due to its high recoil but some players have used this to their advantage by Gun Jumping with the recoil. The ability to Gun Jump using the Mag Blaster is the focus of one of the Arcade Challenges. It can also kill players through walls if one is standing close enough.

The preferred gun for enacting justice in a post-apocalyptic megacity.

Old faithful, the 9MM pistol.
