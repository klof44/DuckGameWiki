Fire Extinguisher

The Fire Extinguisher is a tool featured typically on maps with an abundance of sources of fire-based weapons. Foam shot by the Fire Extinguisher is completely harmless and cannot be used as a weapon. However, the Fire Extinguisher is a valuable resource in fire-based combat, as a burning duck can put themselves out by touching the foam.

Online it will have 4 seconds of ammo capacity instead of 6.66

Safety first! Extinguishes fires but also just makes everything fun and foamy.

Category Fire
Ammo Type Foam
Firing Mode Automatic
Spread High
Direction Straight with gravity
Range 5 tiles
Capacity 6.66 seconds
Console Name fireextinguisher
