Grenade

The Grenade is a common weapon found on many maps. After pulling the pin, a player has 4 seconds before the grenade explodes, and a player may hold onto it to "cook" it. Grenades explode shooting out fragments in a 5 block radius - if one of them hits a player, that player dies.

A Grenade loses its pin when bouncing off a spring.

A player can survive an explosion by having their entire body behind some block(s). Wearing Armor or a helmet and having a fragment strike the armor will stop a single fragment. Shoes do not stop fragments. It is possible to completely avoid an explosion if the affected is lucky.

#1 Pull pin. #2 Throw grenade. Order of operations is important here.

To cook grenade, pull pin and hold until feelings of terror run down your spine. Serves as many ducks as you can fit into a 3 meter radius.
