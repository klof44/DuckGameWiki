Grenade

The Grenade is a common weapon found on many maps. After pulling the pin, a player has 4 seconds before the Grenade explodes, and a player may hold onto it to "cook" it in order to define the range at which they want it to explode. Grenades explode into fragments that span a 5 block radius - if one of them hits a player, that player dies. Grenade's lose their pins when bouncing off a spring.

A player can survive an explosion by having their entire body behind certain Blocks as well as throwing it up to minimize the area of the player's hitbox as the shrapnel expands around them, potentially killing nearby ducks. Wearing Armor or a Helmet and having a fragment strike the armor will stop a single fragment as well as boost the player away from the Grenade. Shoes do not stop fragments. It is possible to completely avoid an explosion if the affected is lucky.

#1 Pull pin. #2 Throw grenade. Order of operations is important here.

To cook grenade, pull pin and hold until feelings of terror run down your spine. Serves as many ducks as you can fit into a 3 meter radius.
