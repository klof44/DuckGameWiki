Fire Crackers

Fire Crackers are rarely seen on official maps and in Random Blocks. When the fire button is pressed, a firecracker is thrown with a small explosion resulting after 2 seconds. If a player is caught in the extremely small explosion radius they will be killed, it is generally very difficult to kill another player with them.

Warning: these are dangerous explosives, not a spicy snack treat.
