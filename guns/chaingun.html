Chaingun

The Chaingun is a charge weapon found on some maps. To fire the Chaingun the fire button must be held to spin-up the Chaingun before firing. If the fire button is released, the Chaingun will not spin down instantly. As fire is sustained, the barrels will glow red and the fire rate will decrease. By not firing, the Chaingun will cool down and return to normal. When held, the player will walk noticeably slower and will be unable to flap their wings, therefore a Jetpack is useful to aid in maneuvering; so useful, in fact, that there is an Arcade Challenge focusing on it.

Like a chaingun, but for adults. Fires mean pointy metal things.
