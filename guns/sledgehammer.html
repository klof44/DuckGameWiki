Sledgehammer

The Sledgehammer is a melee weapon found on numerous official maps. When held, a player will move noticeably slower. When swung, the Sledgehammer will propel the user forward and upward, at the same time releasing a white arc in the players direction. The arc released will destroy certain objects while only moving others, and can travel through walls. If the Sledgehammer is thrown or dropped and hits a players head it will kill them just as a Box or Barrel would. While it is being swung, the Sledgehammer cannot be dropped, and the player wielding it cannot ragdoll either.

See Sledgehammer Techniques for advanced uses.
