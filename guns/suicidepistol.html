Suicide Pistol

The Suicide Pistol is a weapon with the barrel pointed toward the user killing them upon regularly firing the gun. Often the Suicide Pistol will come out of a Random Item Box or Present, leading to the unsuspecting player attempting to spam the fire button in order to attack enemy players, only to be tricked into shooting themselves. Many of the stats are shared with the regular Magnum revolver. Red grips and barrel direction differentiate the Suicide Pistol and the Magnum at first glance. The Suicide Pistol can be fired without killing the player if the player is fluttering while firing, propelling the player increasingly upwards and shooting ducks below themselves. Similarly, holding crouch and firing will cause the player to slide the moment the gun fires and avoid death while killing ducks behind them.
