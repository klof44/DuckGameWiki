Combat Shotgun

The Combat Shotgun is a weapon which is generally effective in most situations. The Combat Shotgun can be considered a direct upgrade to the Shotgun, having more ammunition and a slightly higher range. When shooting, the user must manually pump between shots. Although its bullets do not have piercing properties, Wooden Doors and Boxes will typically be destroyed when shot by the Combat Shotgun due to its high number of bullets per shot. Remaining ammunition can be determined by the number of shotgun shells left on the side of the weapon.
