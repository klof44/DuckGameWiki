Mine

The Mine is a trap weapon left on the ground when the fire button is pressed. If a player steps on an active Mine it will be triggered, if further movement is made the Mine will explode. By throwing an item on the Mine or shooting it, the Mine may explode. Overall, the Mine is fairly inconsistent and unpredictable and sometimes will not trigger when a player is on it, this is likely programmed in.

There is an achievement for becoming the last one remaining while standing on an activated mine.

Once placed in position, explodes if any curious Ducks walk by.
