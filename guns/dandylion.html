Dandylion

The Dandylion is a pellet gun which has an infinite amount of ammo, but only the first 8 shots have high power and thus high range. After the 8th shot, the internal spring fails (a "pling" sound can be heard, and the spring can be seen dangling around), and range is greatly reduced. Pellets shot from the Dandylion bounce from walls. Angling downwards with the Dandylion will reveal that the bullets curve rather significantly midair.

Careful with that thing, you'll lose an eye!
