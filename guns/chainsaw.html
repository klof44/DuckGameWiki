Chainsaw

The Chainsaw is a complex weapon with many techniques to master. Before use the Chainsaw must be started by double tapping the fire button. To kill opponents the fire button must be held or pressed at the time of impact to rev the Chainsaw. By holding w or d-pad up the Chainsaw can be pointed upward.

If a Chainsaw is thrown and lands upside down it will leak Gasoline, which can be lit by normal means or Chainsaw Sliding over it. An alternative version of the Chainsaw is the Turbo Chainsaw.

See Chainsaw Techniques for more information.

The perfect tool for cutting wood or carving decorative ice sculptures.
