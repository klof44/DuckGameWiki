Virtual Shotgun

The Virtual Shotgun is a blue, infinite ammunition variant of the Shotgun. The Virtual Shotgun is often considered the best available weapon on maps it appears on because of its infinite ammunition. Like the other shotguns, the Virtual Shotgun will destroy wooden Doors and Boxes when shot.

The perfect shotgun for life inside a computer simulation. Virtually infinite ammo.
