RC Controller

The RC Controller controls a small RC Car when used. When the fire button is held, the RC Controller controls the RC Car with the usual movement controls. If the fire button is held and the throw/pickup button is pressed, the RC Car will explode in a radius around its location, destroying itself in the process. The RC Car can be blown up using other weapons independent of the RC Controller. After the RC Car has exploded, the RC Controller is mostly useless except for being thrown or saved in Purple Boxes. However, if a player other than the user explodes the RC Car while it's in use, the user can still trigger an explosion at the location it was destroyed.

The RC Controller cannot appear in Random Boxes.

Allows you to drive a small car strapped with explosives. Every Duck's dream!
