WAGNUS

The WAGNUS moves players quickly in their backwards direction, as indicated by the laser light shining out of it, it also works upwards if held at an angle.

Upon collision, it kills other players or damages objects.

When used in mid air, the WAGNUS has two charges after which it must be recharged, this only occurs if the player is not in suspended flight (holding flight button).

The WAGNUS can overheat and explode if used too much.
