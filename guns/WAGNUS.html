WAGNUS

The WAGNUS moves players quickly in the opposite-facing direction, as indicated by the laser light shining out of it, it also works upwards if held at an angle. Upon collision, it kills other players or damages objects. When used in mid air, the WAGNUS has two charges after which it must be recharged, this only occurs if the player is not fluttering.

The WAGNUS can overheat and explode if used too much.

The WAGNUS is also the only gun in the game to produce a different Angle when fired on the same frame as turning, sending the player straight downwards.
