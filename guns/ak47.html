AK47

The AK47 is a prolific weapon which is also featured in Duck Game. High spread and capacity mixed with the automatic nature make the AK47 a popular choice on most maps for which it is featured. The recoil produced will throw the user back and can be very effectively used for Gun Jumping. Tap firing the AK47 will maintain its accuracy. Holding down fire and Alternating is possible though very difficult, it is recommended to conserve the ammo it has to hit your opponent with a straight shot by limiting its use between intervals of combat.

Go-to weapon of all your favorite Duck Action Heroes.
