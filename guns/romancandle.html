Roman Candle

The Roman Candle is a fire weapon found usually on fire-based levels. When the fire button is pressed the Roman Candle is activated and after 1.75 seconds a candle ball will shoot out. Candle balls will continue shooting out every half a second until capacity is reached. Typically the Roman Candle is lit then thrown like a Grenade but it can also be held.

Fireworks, but even more dangerous! Light the fuse and cross your fingers.
