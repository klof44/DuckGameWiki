Turbo Chainsaw

The Turbo Chainsaw is an alternative version of the Chainsaw. One difference between the two is the texture, where the Turbo Chainsaw is blue and green, it is also significantly faster than the Chainsaw and leaves a trail of fire while sliding.

There is a Chancy Challenge with the same name that features this weapon.

In order to create a Turbo Chainsaw in the editor, you need to toggle the souped option on a Chainsaw.
