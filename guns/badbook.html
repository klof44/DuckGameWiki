Bad Book

The Bad Book is an Unavailable Weapon in Duck Game that would have functioned as an alternative to the Good Book. The first charge of the book spawned lightning around the duck. In use it charged up in order to eventually kill all of the ducks on the map. It may have turned all of the players into Tombstones though this is unconfirmed.
