Chaindart

The Chaindart is a weapon similar to the Chaingun but instead of bullets, it fires darts. As fire is sustained the fire rate will decrease and the Chaindart starts shooting flaming darts.

After the Chaindart's barrel catches fire, it will eventually burn and become unusable, though when dropped it will not disappear as an empty gun would.

Like a chaingun, but for babies. Fires safety-capped sponge darts.
