Dueling Pistol

The Dueling Pistol is a common gun in Duck Game, it fires a single short range bullet. No recoil is experienced with the Dueling Pistol. The Dueling Pistol is generally seen as an incredibly skill based weapon due to its low capacity, and this is often reflected in map design. Many maps place Dueling Pistols on the map to provide the player with some form of self-defense until a better weapon can be found.

Due to its limited range, spacing is incredibly important when considering what the opponent has and how you may be able to get to them.

The perfect weapon when a Duck has dishonored your family. One shot only.
