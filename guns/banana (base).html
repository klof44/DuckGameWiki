Banana

The Banana is a trap which is left on the ground for a player to slip on. Unlike the Banana Cluster the banana must be eaten with the fire button then thrown similar to a Grenade.

When a player runs over a banana peel on the ground they will slip in the direction they were running, ragdolling them in the process. Banana peels are best placed where a player might slip into a dangerous area.

A tactically placed banana peel can cause major injuries.
