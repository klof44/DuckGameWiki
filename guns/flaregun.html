Flare Gun

The Flare Gun is a two-shot pistol with incendiary ammunition. When fired a projectile will be launched off in a random direction. When the projectile lands it explodes in a fireball lighting everything hit on fire. If fired upward it will come back down unlike many weapons. This is unlike how a flare gun in real life would behave.

This is one of the guns that cannot spawn golden from a Random Box, Present, or an Item Spawner.

Shoots a flare at long range that spits fire on impact. Fun at parties!

For safety purposes, used to call help. What? No it's not a weapon. NO DON'T USE IT LIKE THAT!
