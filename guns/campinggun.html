Camping Gun

Projectiles fired from the Camping Gun immobilize players in a sleeping bag, similar to the Net Gun, but players can escape quicker.

It is reloaded and fired similarly to a Shotgun or Grenade Cannon, every shot requires two taps of the attack key. Should the Camping Gun catch fire the barrel will eventually burn in the same fashion as the Chaindart and will similarly become unusable, dropping the weapon with a burned barrel however will cause it to disappear.

Designed to get campers into bed quickly.
