Phaser

The Phaser is a small laser pistol which can fire in short range semi-automatic or long range charge. When fired at close range, the weapon is best spammed because of the high spread and fast firing speed. Alternatively, the Phaser can be charged by holding the fire button for a long-range shot which can penetrate Doors - including Vertical Doors.

Whenever not in combat, it is suggested that the player charges the Phaser to increase the number of options available to them. It does not cost any ammunition to hold a charged Phaser.

Like a laser, only...phasery? Hold the trigger to charge a more powerful shot.
