Bazooka

The Bazooka is a projectile weapon. A fired Rocket can kill directly or by splash damage on impact. While reloading the weapon, the player is unable to move, and the reload cannot be cancelled by normal means. There are a few ways to cancel it though: being disarmed by a throwable object, dying, getting hit by a Net, Camping Ball, Magnet, or Dart, and the ground beneath you disappearing. Splash damage has a range of 2-3 tiles and destroys any Block except for Grey Blocks or Framework Blocks. Destroying the environment can be used to access other areas of the map which are not normally accessible. The fired Rocket moves at 26.25 tiles per second.

The Bazooka has a 2 second reload time but repeatedly pressing the fire button while reloading will shorten the time depending on how fast you press the button, down to 0.4 seconds.

Funny name, serious firepower. Launches an explosive missile that can destroy terrain.
