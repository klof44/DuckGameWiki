Quad Laser

The Quad Laser is a weapon which fires an enormous bullet. When firing the Quad Laser the user experiences extreme knockback. An extremely slow moving projectile is fired. The projectile fired by the Quad Laser destroys Doors and most other objects, when hit by the laser bullet the player is turned into a Cooked Duck. The projectile can be deflected when hit with the Sword or Chainsaw, upon being hit, it will speed up. When hit by an Energy Scimitar it creates an Energy Scimitar Blast similar to the Death Ray's laser but blue.

Despite its graphic being enormous, its actual collision size is 2x2.

The Quad Laser is a reference to Aqua Teen Hunger Force which is another property owned by Adult Swim.

Shoots a slow-moving science block of doom that passes through walls.
