Plasma Blaster

The Plasma Blaster is a powerful weapon found on some maps. Extremely high fire rate with some spread make the plasma blaster very powerful. As the user sustains fire the plasma blaster's fire rate decreases and the spread increases, if the user stops shooting there will be a cooldown period before the fire rate and spread returns. Despite being a laser weapon, the shots that are fired are closer to an actual bullet.

The gun is a reference to Earthworm Jim's very Plasma Blaster.

Out of control rapid-fire blaster. Seriously, be careful with this one.

Originally found in a crater next to a burnt power suit. It's origin and mechanism of action are unknown, but tests indicate that it is seriously badass.
