Good Book

The Good Book is an unconventional close range weapon. When close to other players and the fire button is pressed the an area of effect is formed and if any opposing players are caught in it they are temporarily stunned. If the player using the Good Book continues to press the fire button the stunned player will be converted and "join" the attackers team and gain their Hat. If either or both of the two players is alive at the end of the round a point is awarded to the player who converted the other. Converting does not work in the air, both players must be on the ground.

Converting dead ducks will perform a Funeral, creating a Tombstone in the process. Holding Quack while using the Good Book will create the yellow aura around you without converting other ducks. There is an achievement for winning a match by converting all three other players to your team.
