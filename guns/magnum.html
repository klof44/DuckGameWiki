Magnum

The Magnum is a weapon with the ability to penetrate Doors, Boxes and Glass with no penalty. While powerful, the Magnum has the unique challenge of the gun Angling upward with every shot, with it slowly lowering it back to straight over time. The Angling can be a disadvantage because it makes follow-up shots more difficult much of the time. However, Angling can also be advantageous, as easy Angle shots can be achieved hitting targets which would normally be difficult to hit.

Heavy duty pistol that pierces many objects. Cool shades not included.

Standard issue .44 Magnum. Pretty great for killing things, really great for killing things that are trying to hide. Watch the kick, unless you're trying to shoot the ceiling.
