Banana Cluster

Unlike the banana, the Banana Cluster leaves banana peels on the stage with every trigger press. Banana Clusters bounce like grenades when thrown and once 3 banana peels are fired, it will disappear. That said, If a player drops a banana cluster with one banana left on the cluster, it will turn into two normal bananas. So each cluster can potentially fire 4 banana peels.

When a player runs over a banana peel on the ground they will slip in the direction they were running, ragdolling them in the process. Because of this, banana peels are best placed where an enemy player may slip to their death. Sliding over a banana peel or jumping along them will not cause a player to slip. Using a joystick in order to walk very slowly will also prevent slipping, but this isn't as practical and takes more effort.
