Laser Rifle

The Laser Rifle is a laser-based high fire rate weapon, most notable for its downward Angle shots. Lasers shot by the Laser Rifle will ricochet upon hitting a surface. If a player is in a confined space, accidental suicide is likely if the player is not careful. By pointing the rifle upward an Angle shot can be achieved easily. The Laser Rifle is commonly referred to as the "Green Gun" and its use is frowned upon in higher level play.

The Laser Rifle's bullets are guaranteed to go through pipes.

Fires a reflecting beam of deadly energy at an angle. Science makes it happen!
