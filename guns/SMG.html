SMG

The SMG is an uncommon weapon on official maps, akin to a less powerful AK47. Automatic fire and high spread make the SMG a solid choice for close-quarters combat, but its short range makes it undesirable compared to weapons such as the Pistol. The SMG is modeled after the common MP5 sub machine gun.

Rapid-fire bullet-spitting machine. Great for making artisanal swiss cheese.
