Grenade Launcher

The Grenade Launcher is one of the more challenging weapons in Duck Game. If the fire button is held down, the Grenade Launcher will begin to angle upward until it's at 90 degrees from horizontal. Releasing the fire button at any point will fire a launcher grenade from the launcher at the angle it is currently at. When a launcher grenade lands, it explodes in a one block radius. In the first 0.26 seconds of being fired, the launcher grenade will ricochet off of a surface before exploding, this can be quite difficult to predict.

Delivers a fun & exciting present to a long distance friend. Hold fire to adjust arc.
