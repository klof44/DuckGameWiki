Beta Scimitar

The Beta Scimitar is an unused item that behaves differently than the Energy Scimitar. The melee attack carries the duck forward several tiles and will stick the weapon in the wall if the duck dashes into a wall. Pressing down while using the weapon causes it to strike downwards very quickly. The weapon can also launch the duck considerably high if down and throw are pressed while midair.

All of the attacks cause the Beta Scimitar to heat up and will eventaully be too hot for the duck to hold and will cause the duck to drop it or throw it straight horizontally if the duck is running while this happens.
