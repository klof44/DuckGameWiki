Snubby Pistol

The Snubby Pistol is a single-action snub-nose pistol. Seen in a similar role as the Pistol, the snubby pistol is generally effective on most maps. Although it is practical, the Snubby Pistol is usually tossed in favor of other weapons because of its manual firing mode. The low recoil paired with close-range combat makes running spam with the Snubby Pistol extremely effective.

The world's most adorable gun.
