Grenade Cannon

The Grenade Cannon is a weapon that shoots cannon grenades.

Firing the weapon works in two steps: The fire button is first pressed to remove the cannon grenade's locking pin, then the fire button is pressed again to launch the cannon grenade. Similar to the Grenade Launcher, if the fire button is held the Grenade Cannon will begin to angle upward until it's at 90 degrees from horizontal. Releasing the fire button at any point will launch a cannon grenade at the angle the gun is currently at.

If an activated cannon grenade is not fired within 2 seconds the Grenade Cannon unleashes a humongous burst of fire.

An unstable weapon filled with explosions. Spits fire if the trigger is held too long.
