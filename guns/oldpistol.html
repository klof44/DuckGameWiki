Old Pistol

The Old Pistol is a common fire and reload pistol resembling a flintlock pistol.

Unlike other fire and reload weapons, the Old Pistol can be reloaded while moving within 1.5 seconds.

Often the Old Pistol is used in place of the Pistol.

A pain in the tailfeathers to reload, but it'll get the job done.
