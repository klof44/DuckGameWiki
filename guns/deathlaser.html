Death Laser

The Death Laser is an unfinished weapon only found on custom maps. When using the death laser, a bolt is fired which can either kill or revive dead players. To ensure death, the player on the opposing end of the death laser must be thrown off the map. This weapon is ideal for team matches because of the reviving nature of the weapon. The gun will eventually have properties that transmit the coil laser through Wires and will be able to remotely activate items based around them as well as Spawn Cannons and Laser Spawners.

In the level editor it is described as "OFFLINE ONLY".
