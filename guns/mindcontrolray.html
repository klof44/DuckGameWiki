Mind Control Ray

The Mind Control Ray is a weapon that emits a ray that does not directly kill opponents. The emitted ray travels through walls and portals. When another player is caught in range of the ray, the player using the mind control ray is given control of the other player for a brief period of time.

When another player is being controlled by the mind control ray, the user is immobilised. If the user quacks while another player is being controlled, the effects of the mind control ray will end.

A player wearing a tinfoil hat will not be affected by the mind control ray.

Best friend of Mind Control Steve. Takes control of enemy Ducks.
