Keytar

The Keytar is a musical instrument introduced with the Duck Game 1.5 update. It can have one of the five colors blue, green, red, yellow or pink (1/101 chance on spawn).

It has 5 presets that can be switched through (on controller: L1) and two ways of modifying the pitch or tone (L2, R2). If the jam button is pressed (default: Home), the Keytar can be played by pressing number keys and backspace on a keyboard.

Eats batteries and steals hearts.
