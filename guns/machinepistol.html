Machine Pistol

Extreme rapid fire, high spread with a fairly large capacity makes the Machine Pistol extremely deadly at close range. Similarly the rapid fire depletes the ammo magazine very quickly. There is little push back recoil but when firing it makes it difficult for the player to move.

Need to deliver a bunch of bullets to someone in a hurry? Try this.

Old faithful, the 9MM pistol.
