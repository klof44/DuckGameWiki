Boxing Gloves

The Boxing Gloves is an unavailable weapon in Duck Game that currently has no use, it cannot be equipped and has no function. The weapon is meant to knock out ducks through use of the fire action and ragdoll them with momentum away from the user, however this was not implemented as it was believed players would get confused as to whether or not they had died as a result of this action.
