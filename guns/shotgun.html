Shotgun

The Shotgun is a very common short-range weapon that appears on many maps. When shot, it releases 6 shells with a high spread, making the Shotgun a solid choice for basic self-defense. The Shotgun must be pumped in between shots to reload.

If shot at point-blank, wooden Doors and Boxes will be destroyed, allowing 1 and 2 shells through respectively. This makes it a highly preferable choice over other non-penetrating weapons when either of these lies between players.
