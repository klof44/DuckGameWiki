Sniper

The Sniper is a bolt-action rifle with a laser sight. With long range, no spread, and the ability to penetrate most props, the Sniper is seen as one of the best long range weapons. When the Sniper is ready to fire the laser beam can be seen, when unloaded no beam can be seen. Most maps featuring the Sniper have large open areas which allow for long sight lines. The most direct competition to the sniper is the Magnum which shares many of its traits. The Sniper has a stronger counterpart called the Sharpshot.

Long range rifle - equipped with a laser sight so you look real cool.
